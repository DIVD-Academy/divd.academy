At DIVD.Academy, we are always looking for passionate and talented individuals to join our team. Our organization offers a variety of positions in areas such as cybersecurity research, software development, education, and community engagement. We seek candidates who are dedicated to making the digital world safer and more secure. By joining DIVD.Academy, you will be part of a dynamic and innovative team that is committed to excellence and continuous learning. At DIVD.Academy, our mission is to enhance knowledge, skills, and ethical awareness in cybersecurity while fostering inclusivity and connecting young talent. We provide a safe and collaborative environment for both novice and experienced researchers to learn from each other, creating a unique niche in 21st-century IT education, research, and security. Recognizing the growing interest in cybersecurity and the need for proper training, we aim to guide young talent who may lack digital boundaries and ethical understanding. Our strategy focuses on training the next generation of hackers and raising cybersecurity awareness, ensuring they are well-prepared for the digital world while promoting a culture of inclusivity and diversity.

Ethics committee

As a member of the Ethics Committee at the DIVD Academy foundation, you actively contribute to promoting ethical standards and values in the broad domain of digital security, preventing the abuse of vulnerabilities, and safeguarding the Code of Conduct. Your role is essential in assessing ethical issues related to digital vulnerabilities, disclosure, and research practices, as well as understanding the synergy between ethics and cybersecurity.

Head of Education

We are searching for a head of Education to develop new curricula across various disciplines in the DIVD academy. The head of Education must improve teaching practices through collaboration with staff, volunteers, and students. You should be able to use new and existing resources to ensure student needs are met and educational goals are achieved. You will authorize textbook acquisitions and, on occasion, be tasked with writing your educational materials.

To be successful as a head of Education, you should stay up-to-date with pedagogical theories and developments in Education. An exceptional head of Education should have superb people skills and be confident in engaging individuals from various backgrounds and disciplines. You should be energetic, passionate, and able to communicate objectives.

Responsibilities:

  • Developing new curricula with the help of teachers, students, and other staff.

  • Networking and building professional relationships with the volunteers of the academy and the DIVD community.

  • Monitoring the progress of new and existing teaching methods.

  • Closely evaluate results at the end of each quarter and write progress reports.

  • Supervising the recruitment and vetting of prospective teachers.

  • Identifying areas for improvement and scheduling workshops for new and existing staff.

Education Director Requirements:

  • A solid ethical code.

  • First-class organizational skills.

  • Maintain a positive attitude and be open to feedback.

  • Teaching certification and previous teaching experience.

  • The ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously.

  • The ability and patience to draft several documents and policies.

  • The ability to use tact and diplomacy in interactions with all relevant parties.

Nice to have:

  • Speaks fluently in Dutch.

  • Excellent written and verbal communications skills

  • Customer-service oriented with a problem-solving attitude.

  • Master's degree in education administration or a related field.

  • Excellent interpersonal skills with a high level of professionalism

What do we offer

Wherever your personal goal is to make your routine as a volunteer impactful, enjoyable, exciting, and comfortable, you can choose if you want to work remotely or from our office in the Netherlands. You'll receive a set of benefits to improve your work experience. They include a flexible schedule, a multicultural environment with colleagues in the Netherlands, a vibrant and dynamic organizational culture, and free access to our online learning platform as you help build a better society.

Internship / Trainee supervisor

We are looking for a reliable and trustworthy internship/trainee supervisor to join our team. You will assist with supporting young people in our internship and trainee program. As a supervisor, you will deal with young people aged 16-25, guiding them through the institute with the hiring head of the department where they enrolled in the job position.

Responsibilities

  • Support the Head of Human Resources in the planning, developing, and executing of the internship and trainee program.
     Develop an internal internship/trainee strategy and work with the team to execute the plan.

  • Provide invaluable advice to the interns and trainees, collaborate with leadership to enhance the internship and trainee program, and embed practical internship and trainee engagement into the everyday.

Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent or demonstrable experience (thinking/working level) with additional knowledge in the Technical ICT or management training field.

  • Experience with interns and trainee is a plus.

  • A security clearance to work with younger people.

Internship / Trainee supervisors [Dutch]

We zijn altijd op zoek naar enthousiaste stage-/trainee supervisors om ons team in het Trainee Office te versterken. Je zult helpen bij de ondersteuning van jongeren in ons stage- of traineeprogramma. Als supervisor zul je werken met jongeren in de leeftijd van 16-25 jaar en hen begeleiden binnen het kantoor of op afstand, in samenwerking met het hoofd van de afdeling waar ze zijn aangenomen.

Verantwoordelijkheden

  • Ondersteuning bieden aan het hoofd van People and Culture bij de planning, ontwikkeling en uitvoering van het stage- en traineeprogramma.

  • Ontwikkelen van een interne strategie voor het stage-/trainee-programma en samenwerken met het team om het plan uit te voeren.

  • Onmisbare adviezen verstrekken aan de stagiairs en trainees, samenwerken met collega's om het stage- en traineeprogramma voortdurend te verbeteren en praktische betrokkenheid van stagiairs en trainees in het dagelijks leven te integreren.

**Belangrijkste verantwoordelijkheden
 **

  • Begeleiding, ondersteuning en mentorship bieden aan trainees binnen DIVD.Academy.

  • Ontwerpen en implementeren van trainingsprogramma's ter ondersteuning van de professionele ontwikkeling van trainees.

  • Toezicht houden op en evalueren van de prestaties van trainees, waarbij constructieve feedback en begeleiding worden geboden.

  • Zorgen voor naleving van het kantoorbeleid en ethische normen.

  • Trainees helpen bij het oplossen van problemen en het overwinnen van werkgerelateerde uitdagingen.

Vereisten

  • Eerdere ervaring in mentoring, coaching of begeleiding van individuen (bij voorkeur in een educatieve of professionele omgeving).

  • Uitstekende communicatieve en interpersoonlijke vaardigheden.

  • Bereid zijn om sterke organisatorische en leiderschapskwaliteiten te ontwikkelen.

  • Begrip van trainingsmethodologien en principes van leerontwikkeling.

  • Toewijding aan het ondersteunen van de groei en ontwikkeling van anderen.

  • Mogelijkheid om minimaal 1 uur per week als vrijwilliger te werken vanuit het Trainee Office (op locatie of online).

We gebruiken Slack om met de studenten te communiceren en hebben twee vaste locaties in Den Haag op 5 minuten afstand van Den Haag Centraal. En we zijn bezig met meerdere locaties in Nederland.

Voordelen

  • De mogelijkheid om bij te dragen aan de professionele ontwikkeling van aspirerende individuen.

  • Versterk je leiderschaps- en mentorvaardigheden in een dynamische, ondersteunende omgeving.

  • Certificaat van erkenning voor je waardevolle bijdrage als Trainee Supervisor bij DIVD.Academy.

Als je gepassioneerd bent over het mentoren en ondersteunen van de groei van toekomstige professionals, meld je dan aan als vrijwilliger en geef hier aan dat je wilt solliciteren voor deze rol.

Sluit je aan bij ons om de volgende generatie professionals bij DIVD.Academy te versterken! Jouw toewijding en begeleiding zullen een aanzienlijke impact hebben op hun weg naar succes.
